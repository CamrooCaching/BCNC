Dark was the Night...

Combination for lock on final: Up, Left, Left, Right -- I'd recommend writing this down!



The low-lying lands you stand on right now have overtime become a floodplain of this meandering section of Big Creek. During seasons of heavy rainfall or ice melt, this whole area will find itself submerged under mass amounts of water. This is not an area you'd want to be in during a heavy storm. On that note, you may want to make your way back to the trailhead as it seems a thunderstorm may be approaching. Seek shelter at the final coordinates which will be found by clicking continue!
