Dark was the Night...

As you look to your left and your right down Mulligan Road, you notice some deer tracks in the ground and take a closer look. Whitetail Deer are one of the most common animals found here, with over 400,000 across all of Ontario, sighting one of these creatures is a common occurrence.
You make your way NE down the road until you reach a dead end. The road once went through here, but over time the bridge fell into disrepair and was eventually removed. Big Creek now stands mostly untouched as nature finds its way back into this area. When you approach the end of the road you'll see the glow of the checkerboard, on the back you should see a barcode with the text "County of Brant" above it.
